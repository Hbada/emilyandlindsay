The Adventurers

Emily

Clever kid with type 1 diabetes. Curious about everything, and athletic as much as possible. Loves reading, games, and pet-sitting.

Super power: speed in all things physical and mental.

Weakness: Boredom makes her do strange things.

Lindsay

Emily's best friend. Athletic and adventurous, like Emily. Loves activities at the park, and helping Emily solve problems.

Super power: flexibility in body and mind.

Weakness: Distracted by boys, which irritates Emily.
